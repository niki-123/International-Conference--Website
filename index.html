About Institute


G H Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE) Nagpur, established in 1996, is an autonomous institution affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University. GHRCE ranked 139th PAN-India in Engineering Discipline as declared by NIRF Ranking 2020. GHRCE is ranked 2nd in the country by Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) 2020, Govt. of India. The institute has dual accreditation: National Board of Accreditation (NBA) Tier-I (Washington Accord) and NAAC A+ (3.51/4) grade by National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) during 2nd cycle.

With emphasis on Research & Development, institute has to its credit, more than 5000 publications, 49 books and books chapters, and 9000 plus citations including in Web of Science, Scopus and Google Scholar, 25 copyrights, 211 patents filed (5 granted, 152 Published, 97 under Examination) by faculty and students and is consistently ranked in top 10 patent filers in India from last five years as per the Indian Patent Office report.

About Conference

International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Computing Applications -21 (ICCICA-21) will be held in G H Raisoni College of Engineering Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, on 18th to 19th June 2021. The main objective of the conference is to bring together the researchers from the academia, research laboratories, and stakeholders from the industry. The conference will facilitate the presentation of the recent developments and exchange of the practical experience in the field of Computational Intelligence. The conference will encourage the discussion, dissemination of information, exchange of ideas, and collaboration among the participants.

Organized by:

Department of Computer Science & Engineering , Artificial Intelligence and Information Technology, GHRCE and GHR Labs & Research Centre, India
